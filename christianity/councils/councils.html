  3. The Council of Ephesus (431 AD)
  4. The Council of Chalcedon (451 AD)
  5. The Second Council of Constantinople (553 AD)
  6. The Third Council of Constantinople (680-681 AD)
  7. The Second Council of Nicaea (787 AD)
